I’m excited to be co-organising the **Future Wind and Marine 2027** conference, which will be hosted in Oxford. The event will showcase the work and impact of the *Wind and Marine Energy Systems and Structures Centre for Doctoral Training*, highlighting research across offshore wind, marine energy, and related technologies.
![Future Wind and Marine Conference](/images/FWAM_2020_pic.jpg)

The conference will bring together researchers, industry partners, and students to share ideas, present cutting-edge work, and explore the future of sustainable energy systems.

*If you're interested in wind and marine energy, it should be a great event to follow!*
